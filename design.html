Problem Statement

Background

Newton Creek is a covered creek in a the Norfolk area that experiences significant flooding from tidal and rain events. A daylighting project has the potential to create a blue-green space that would address flooding, create habitat, and a community park. The project also involves a retreat strategy to relocate roads as sea levels rise.

Problem Statement

Historic development in the St. Paul redevelopment area of Norfolk has led to flooding, especially during periods of high intensity precipitation events. This flooding has significant and detrimental effects on the residences of this area. Moreover, this area is forecasted to be a safe haven against future flooding because of its increased elevation relative to the area around it. The needs of future residents should also be considered for any potential design plans. A good redesign of this area has the potential to address the needs of the local residents, ecosystems, and economies. If no action is taken, the area will continue to flood, and this flooding will only intensify due to changing climatic conditions.

Design Criteria and Constraints

Design Criteria

  • Attenuate stormwater and storm surge peaks by 20% for a 100 year flood
  • Maintain a functional design life of 20 years given the projected sea level rise of .41 meters and the associated increase in salinity during that time.
  • Create a plant community with a Shannon Diversity Index of at least 2.5
  • Reduce Total Suspended Solids (TSS) by 20%
  • Remain within a $2,000,000 budget

Design Constraints

  • Preserve the Gethsemane Community Fellowship Church and McDemmond Center for Applied Research
  • Easily maintained by local maintenance crews
  • Minimal modification to existing transportation infrastructure (railroad, highway, parking lot)
